MAKKAH AND MADINAH

Makkah and Madinah are my dream destinations to visit with my family and future partner, if I am blessed with the opportunity. These are the holiest cities in Islam, where Muslims turn in prayer, and they hold great historical significance from the time of the Prophet Muhammad and his companions. I also hope to visit sacred places such as Jabal al-Noor, and the Rawdah which is described in Hadith as a garden from the gardens of Paradise.

MANCHESTER

Manchester has always been a dream destination for me after Makkah and Madinah. I am deeply drawn to the city’s architectural beauty and its artistic urban design, where every corner seems to reflect a touch of fine detail and history. My interest in Manchester began when a close friend started studying at the University of Manchester. Through her daily posts, I was constantly exposed to the city’s unique atmosphere and vibrant lifestyle.

NORWAY

Norway is a dream destination that always holds a special place in my heart because of its unique landscape wonders, especially the Aurora Borealis phenomenon that often adorns its night sky with mesmerising dances of green and purple lights. As a nature lover, I long for the experience of snow-covered mountains while witnessing this visual symphony of nature firsthand. The tranquillity of the winter atmosphere in Norway not only promises an aesthetic view but also offers an extraordinary peace of mind. Norway is a "magical" location that I must visit at least once in my lifetime.
